White Cookies

  1. 1 stick butter
  2. 2 tbs. sugar
  3. 1 cup flour
  4. 1/2 cup pecans
  5. Powdered sugar to coat

Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla. Add flour and mix, then pecans.

Bake at 325 for about an hour (check after 45 minutes). Completely cool before shaking in a bag with powder sugar.

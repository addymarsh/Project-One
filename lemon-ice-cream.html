Lemon Ice Cream

  1. 1 1/2 Cups lemon juice
    (12 oz. - I use the frozen Minute Maid bottles)
  2. 4 cups of sugar
  3. 1 tbsp. grated lemon rind optional
  4. 1 cup whipping cream
  5. 6 cup whole milk

Combine lemon juice and sugar; Stir well. Let stand 30 min. Add lemon rind, whipping cream, and milk; mix well. Pour into freezer can and freeze according to directions. Let ripen 1 1/2 to 2 hours; Spoon into serving dishes.

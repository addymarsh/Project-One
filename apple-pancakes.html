Apple Pancakes

  1. Use any pancake mix
  2. Chop up an apple into thin slices
  3. Butter up your frying pan
  4. Add some of the apple slices
  5. Pour in the pancake mix
  6. Add some more apple slices on top
  7. cook the pancakes!

note: this is an unperfected recipe :(
